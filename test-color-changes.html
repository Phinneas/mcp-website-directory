Summary

The new Warm Earth color scheme replaces the previous purple/amethyst theme with a warmer, more inviting palette featuring:

  • Primary: Warm Orange (#EE7B30) - energetic and attention-grabbing
  • Secondary: Warm Beige (#D1B490) - natural and comforting
  • Accent: Muted Teal (#8AA8A1) - sophisticated and balanced
  • Tertiary: Cool Lavender-Gray (#CBCBD4) - subtle and professional
