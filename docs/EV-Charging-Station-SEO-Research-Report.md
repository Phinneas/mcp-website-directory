The EV charging station market shows **massive search volume** (823K+ monthly searches) with **moderate competition**. Clear opportunities exist for businesses that can target high-intent commercial and transactional queries while addressing user concerns about cost, location, and charging speed.

## 15. Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Competition Risks

**Risk:** Government sites (Energy.gov) dominate for informational queries
**Mitigation:** Focus on commercial/transactional content they don't provide

**Risk:** Well-funded competitors (ChargePoint, Electrify America) have huge SEO teams
**Mitigation:** Target long-tail niches, local markets, and specific use cases

### Market Risks

**Risk:** EV market slowdown could reduce search volume
**Mitigation:** Diversify into supply chain, installation services, consulting

**Risk:** New charging standards (NACS vs CCS) create confusion
**Mitigation:** Position as educational authority; create comparison content

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** API dependencies for real-time data
**Mitigation:** Build fallback static content; cache aggressively

**Risk:** Google algorithm updates target local pack
**Mitigation:** Diversify traffic sources; build direct brand traffic
